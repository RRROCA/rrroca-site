Questions

Membership FAQ

Quick answers about how RRROCA memberships and payments work.

Do I need a paid membership to receive RRROCA news?

No. Resident updates, alerts, and the newsletter are open to everyone in Rocky Ridge and Royal Oak.

How do I pay for a membership?

RRROCA can use Stripe Payment Links, which open a secure hosted checkout page with no custom payment code required on the website.

What is the difference between Household and Family memberships?

Household memberships are ideal for one household membership record, while Family memberships extend the same benefits across your full family.

Who should I contact about business memberships or help with signup?

Please use the contact form and select Membership or Sponsorship so a RRROCA volunteer can follow up.
