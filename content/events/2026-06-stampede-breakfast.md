Pull on your boots and join us for one of RRROCA's favourite traditions. The Annual Stampede Breakfast brings neighbours together for pancakes, live music, kids' activities, and a cheerful kick-off to summer in Rocky Ridge and Royal Oak.

Volunteers and sponsors from across the community help make this event feel warm, welcoming, and distinctly local.
