## RRROCA Community Clean Up — Saturday, June 20, 2026

Join your neighbours for our annual **Community Clean Up Day**! Help keep Rocky Ridge and Royal Oak beautiful.

**When:** Saturday, June 20, 2026 — **9:00 AM to 2:00 PM**

**Where:** [8888 Country Hills Blvd NW, Calgary, AB T3G 5T4](https://maps.google.com/?q=8888+Country+Hills+Blvd+NW+Calgary+AB+T3G+5T4)

### What to Bring

- Yard waste, small household items, and general refuse
- Visit the [City of Calgary acceptable items list](https://www.calgary.ca/communities/community-cleanup-items.html) for full details

### ⚠️ Important: No U-Haul Trucks or Big Household Items

This event is for residential community cleanup only. Large items and commercial loads cannot be accepted.
