Help us freshen up our shared green spaces after winter. Volunteers will pick up litter, tidy pathways, and help with light planting around the park and nearby gathering areas.

Bring work gloves, a refillable water bottle, and your community spirit. Cleanup supplies and a thank-you snack station will be provided on site.
