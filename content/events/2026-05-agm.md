Join your neighbours and board members for RRROCA's Annual General Meeting. We'll review the past year, share upcoming priorities, elect directors, and hear ideas from residents about parks, programming, and community safety.

Coffee, tea, and light refreshments will be available starting at 6:30 PM. All residents are welcome, and current members may vote during the meeting.
