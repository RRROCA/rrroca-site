Join your neighbours and board members for RRROCA's **Annual General Meeting** in May 2026.

This evening meeting is open to all Rocky Ridge and Royal Oak residents. We will share community updates, talk through current priorities, and hold voting for board positions.

## Meeting details

- **Date:** Thursday, May 28, 2026
- **Time:** 7:00 PM to 8:45 PM
- **Location:** William D. Pratt School Learning Commons
- **Address:** 8655 Royal Oak Way NW, Calgary, AB

Coffee, tea, and light refreshments will be available starting at **6:30 PM**.

## On the agenda

- Board and committee updates
- Community safety highlights
- Parks and playground discussions
- 2026 event planning
- Election of board positions
- Resident questions and ideas

Whether you are a longtime volunteer or simply curious about what is happening in the community, we would love to welcome you.
