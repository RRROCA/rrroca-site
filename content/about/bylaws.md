## RRRO ByLaws

The Rocky Ridge Royal Oak Community Association recently celebrated our 10th anniversary. Over the past number of years it has become clear that the Bylaws of the Association, while appropriate at the time the organization was launched, no longer meet the needs of the Association and the community we serve.

Consequently the Board has analyzed the Bylaws and proposed a number of changes that are aimed at the effective and sustainable operation of the Association.

**These changes were passed at the AGM Meeting held on November 27, 2017.
