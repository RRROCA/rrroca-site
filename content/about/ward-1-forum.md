## Ward 1 Forum

**PUBLIC SERVICE ANNOUNCEMENT – MUNICIPAL ELECTION**

The community associations of Bowness, Crestmont, Rocky Ridge/Royal Oak, Scenic Acres, Silver Springs, Tuscany, Valley Ridge and Varsity are pleased to host a WARD 1 ALL-CANDIDATES FORUM.

The FORUM will be held WEDNESDAY, OCTOBER 11, 7:30 PM at the Tuscany Club, 212 Tuscany Way NW. The event will be videotaped and will be available online afterwards.

This will provide an opportunity for our residents to see and hear the candidates and to understand their respective platforms and local perspectives. A series of questions will be posed to each candidate who will then provide comment. In addition, there will be tables set up for campaign volunteers to provide material and engage on an individual basis.

The new WARD 1 boundaries include the following communities: Bowness, Crestmont, Greenwood Village/Green Briar, Rocky Ridge, Royal Oak, Scenic Acres, Silver Springs, Tuscany/Lynx Ridge, Valley Ridge and Varsity.

We appreciate the participation of candidates, as this assists constituents to make informed decisions on Election Day, October 16th.

Questions for candidates must be relevant (of municipal concern) and answerable by all candidates. These can be directed to [palmerjr@telus.net](mailto:palmerjr@telus.net), and will be considered by the moderator.
