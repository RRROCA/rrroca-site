# Community Clean Up Returns on June 20

Grab your gloves and join your neighbours! RRROCA's annual **Community Clean Up** is coming up on **Saturday, June 20, 2026**, and it is one of those simple community days that makes Rocky Ridge and Royal Oak feel connected, cared for, and active.

We will be welcoming residents from **9:00 AM to 2:00 PM** at **[8888 Country Hills Blvd NW](https://maps.google.com/?q=8888+Country+Hills+Blvd+NW+Calgary)**. Bring your accepted household items, wave to a few familiar faces in line, and help keep our neighbourhoods looking their best heading into summer.

## Event details

- **Date:** Saturday, June 20, 2026
- **Time:** 9:00 AM to 2:00 PM
- **Location:** 8888 Country Hills Blvd NW

Please note that **U-Haul trucks and big household items will not be accepted** at this event. To avoid a wasted trip, check Calgary's full **[acceptable items list](https://www.calgary.ca/communities/community-cleanup-items.html)** before loading up your vehicle.
