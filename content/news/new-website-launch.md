# RRROCA Launches New Community Website

We are excited to share that the new RRROCA website is now live.

Rebuilt from scratch by local volunteers, the new site is faster, easier to use, and designed to work smoothly whether you are checking in from a desktop, tablet, or phone. It is a fresh new home for Rocky Ridge and Royal Oak residents to stay informed, connected, and involved in the community.

## What you will find

The new website brings together the information residents look for most often in one place. You will find community news, upcoming event listings, safety updates and resources, sports and programs information, and a growing business directory featuring local Rocky Ridge and Royal Oak businesses.

Whether you are looking for the next neighbourhood event, trying to find information about a program, or hoping to support a nearby business, the new site is built to make that easier.

## Transparent governance

One of the most important parts of the new website is our commitment to transparency.

RRROCA board motions and votes are now published publicly for residents to review. We believe this is what transparent governance looks like: clear decisions, visible records, and open communication with the community we serve.

## Built by volunteers, at zero cost

This entire project was built by volunteers using modern open-source tools. That means the new website was created at zero cost to the association, saving RRROCA thousands of dollars compared with the previous WordPress hosting setup.

It is a great example of neighbours contributing their time and skills to build something useful for everyone.
