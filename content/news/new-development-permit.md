# New Development Permit Application





The Mad Dog Pub in the plaza and office building behind Shoppers Drug Mart has an existing outdoor patio on the corner of the restaurant facing 112 Ave. This application is for adding a glass walled and roofed patio enclosure on the same footprint as the existing patio. The proposal does not affect parking, landscaping or any other site components.
