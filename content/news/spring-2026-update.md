# Spring 2026 Board Update

Spring always brings a fresh burst of energy to Rocky Ridge and Royal Oak, and this year is no exception. Your RRROCA board has been busy planning events, reviewing neighbourhood priorities, and finding more ways to keep residents informed and connected.

## AGM coming up this May

Our **Annual General Meeting** is just around the corner, and we would love to see a strong turnout from residents across both neighbourhoods. The AGM is where we share updates, elect board members, and hear directly from the community about what matters most. Visit our [events page](/events/) for details.

## Playground conversations are underway

We have started a new round of discussions about future playground equipment improvements and upgrades. Residents have told us they want spaces that feel fun, inclusive, and durable for the long term, and those conversations are helping shape the next steps.

## Safety committee update

The RRROCA Safety Committee continues to meet regularly, and we are pleased to report that community crime is **down 26% year-over-year**. Rocky Ridge and Royal Oak remain among Calgary's safer neighbourhoods, and that progress reflects strong neighbour awareness, good reporting, and close coordination with Calgary Police Service. Residents can now also use our [online reporting form](/safety/report/) to share non-emergency concerns with the association.

## Stampede Breakfast planning is in full swing

Planning is already underway for another welcoming, family-friendly **Stampede Breakfast** this summer. Expect pancakes, community spirit, and plenty of opportunities to reconnect with neighbours after a busy spring season. Watch our [events page](/events/) for the full announcement.

## Our business directory is launching

We are excited to be preparing the launch of a new RRROCA business directory to help residents discover and support local services close to home. From family-run businesses to trusted neighbourhood professionals, the directory will make it easier to shop local and stay connected.

## Thank you for helping keep RRROCA active

Every event attended, concern reported, volunteer shift filled, and idea shared helps strengthen this community. Thank you for being part of what makes Rocky Ridge and Royal Oak such a welcoming place to live.
