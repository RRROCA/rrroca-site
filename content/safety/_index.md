Rocky Ridge and Royal Oak consistently rank among Calgary's safest communities, with crime rates **well below the city average**. Our Safety Hub helps you stay informed, prepared, and connected.

The chart below compares quarterly crime incidents in Rocky Ridge and Royal Oak against the Calgary community average. Our neighbourhoods typically see **2-4 incidents per quarter** versus the city average of **17-25**.

## Community Liaison Officer

Your Community Liaison Officer (CLO) is your point of contact with Calgary Police Service for neighbourhood concerns:

- **District 7 — Country Hills**: [11955 Country Village Link NE](https://maps.google.com/?q=11955+Country+Village+Link+NE+Calgary)
- **Non-emergency line**: [403-266-1234](tel:4032661234)
- **Online reporting**: [CPS Online Reports](https://www.calgarypolice.ca/our-services/online-services/)

CLOs focus on identifying and resolving neighbourhood issues — speeding, suspicious activity, property crime trends, and more.

- **Block Connectors** — become a point of contact for your block
- **Report concerns** — use our [concern form](/safety/report/) or contact CPS directly

## Road Safety Reminders

- School and playground zones: **30 km/h** when signs are active
- Slow to **60 km/h** when passing emergency vehicles or tow trucks
- Report suspected impaired drivers to **911** with licence plate and description
- Collisions over $2,000 combined damage must be reported to police
- Cyclists have the same rights as vehicles — share the road courteously
