  <div class="report-triage-card report-triage-rrroca">

    <h3>Community-Specific Issue?</h3>
    <p>If your concern is <strong>specific to Rocky Ridge or Royal Oak</strong> and <em>not</em> something Calgary 311 handles — such as recurring suspicious activity, neighbourhood safety patterns, community park issues, or concerns for the RRROCA board — use the form below.</p>
    <p class="report-triage-note-inline"><strong>If 311 covers it, report it there.</strong> They have the resources and authority to act. Only use this form for issues that fall outside city services.</p>
  </div>

  | Graffiti on city property | Graffiti/vandalism on community property |
