RRROCA encourages adults in Rocky Ridge and Royal Oak to stay active year-round.

While dedicated RRROCA adult fitness classes are not currently scheduled, residents can still find excellent local options through nearby recreation providers:

- [Shane Homes YMCA at Rocky Ridge](https://www.ymcacalgary.org/locations/rocky-ridge-ymca)
- [City of Calgary Recreation programs](https://liveandplay.calgary.ca/REGPROG/public/category/browse/ALLPROGRAMS)
- [Community sports clubs and leagues](/sports/sport-clubs/)

If you would like RRROCA to organize future adult fitness programming, please contact [programs@rrroca.org](mailto:programs@rrroca.org).
