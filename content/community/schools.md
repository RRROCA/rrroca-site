## RRROCA and Schools

RRROCA is the link between our local schools and our community. We aim to integrate our schools and children into the community to best serve each other. RRROCA meets with local schools regularly and advocates on their behalf and on behalf of our community to the city and province. We hope to play a role in ensuring our children (and leaders of tomorrow) are active contributors to the betterment of our community.

RRROCA recognizes and interviews an outstanding child in our community each month in our newsletter. We also hold a Kid-Preneur Marketplace to promote and support our children entrepreneurs. RRROCA is also continually seeking new ways to further promote and integrate our schools into the broader community.
