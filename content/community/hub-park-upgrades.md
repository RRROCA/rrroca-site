## About the Project

RRROCA has secured a **Neighbourhood Improvement Fund (NIF) grant** from the City of Calgary to fund professional upgrades to The Hub Park — the heart of our community's outdoor recreation space.

The NIF program supports community-driven projects that improve neighbourhood livability. Our grant is funding professional advisory services from **Spencer Design** to guide the design review and consultant coordination for park improvements.

### What's Being Upgraded

- Professional design review and master planning for the park
- Coordination with engineering and landscape consultants
- Improved park amenities aligned with community feedback
- Upgrades guided by best practices in community park design

### Project Budget

The City of Calgary NIF grant covers professional advisory services up to **$5,760**, with Spencer Design providing design review, coordination, and advisory support on a milestone basis.
