## Borrow from Your Neighbours

Why buy something you'll only use once? RRROCA is exploring a **community sharing program** that lets residents borrow and lend items like tools, sports equipment, camping gear, party supplies, and more — right within Rocky Ridge and Royal Oak.

### How It Works

We're evaluating [JustBorro](https://justborro.com) as a platform for peer-to-peer sharing in our community. The concept is simple:

1. **List items** you're willing to lend or rent out
2. **Browse** what your neighbours have available
3. **Borrow or rent** — arrange pickup and return within the neighbourhood
4. **Save money** and reduce waste by sharing instead of buying

### CA Equipment Rentals

RRROCA owns event equipment that sits idle most of the year. We're exploring making items available for member rentals — providing convenience for you and generating modest revenue to fund community programs:

- 🎪 **Event tents and canopies**
- 🪑 **Tables and chairs** for backyard gatherings
- 🔊 **Sound equipment** for block parties
- ⚽ **Sports equipment** for neighbourhood games

*Equipment rental details and pricing are being finalized. Members will get priority access and discounted rates.*

### Why Community Sharing?

| Benefit | Impact |
|---------|--------|
| 💰 **Save money** | Why buy a pressure washer for one weekend? |
| 🌱 **Reduce waste** | Fewer items manufactured, shipped, and landfilled |
| 🤝 **Build connections** | Meet neighbours you might never have talked to |
| 🏠 **Declutter** | Earn from items sitting in your garage |
| 💵 **Fund community** | Equipment rental fees support RRROCA programs |
