# Join Us!

### Help us build a FUN, SAFE, and FRIENDLY community!

We need your help with funds to continue dealing with municipal and provincial issues that affect your daily life. Help us to continue bringing events and activities to our community!

### Why Support us? So many reasons!

Read more about What we do, Who we are and What has RRROCA been involved in [HERE](https://rrroca.org/en/about-rrroca-calgary/)

### Volunteering

Check Volunteer Opportunities

If you want to help make a difference in your community; DO IT!!! Join us and see how you can help!

We are always looking for volunteers to support events and general activities of the Association. We would love to know more about your interests and the time you may be able to invest with us. Please email [volunteer@rrroca.org](mailto:volunteer@rrroca.org) for more information.

### Sponsor Us

We are looking for local businesses that are willing to participate in our “Discounts for Members” program. Please email: [sponsorship@rrroca.org](mailto:sponsorship@rrroca.org)

**Help us build a Fun, Safe, and Friendly Community!**
