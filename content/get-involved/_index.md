# Get Involved with RRROCA

### Help us build a FUN, SAFE, and FRIENDLY community!

We need your help with funds to continue dealing with municipal and provincial issues that affect your daily life. Help us to continue bringing events and activities to our community!

### Why Support Us?

Read more about [What we do, Who we are, and What RRROCA has been involved in](/about/).

### Volunteering

Want to make a difference? [Check out our volunteer opportunities](/get-involved/volunteer/) and see how you can help!

We are always looking for volunteers to support events and general activities of the Association. Email [volunteer@rrroca.org](mailto:volunteer@rrroca.org) for more information.

### Book a Facility

Need a space for your event or activity? [Book a facility](https://rrroca.getcommunal.com/facilities) through our community portal.

### Sponsor Us

We are looking for local businesses that are willing to participate in our "Discounts for Members" program. Please email [sponsorship@rrroca.org](mailto:sponsorship@rrroca.org).

**Help us build a Fun, Safe, and Friendly Community!**
