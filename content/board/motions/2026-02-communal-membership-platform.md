## Motion Text

**BE IT RESOLVED THAT** the Rocky Ridge Royal Oak Community Association adopt Communal as its membership management platform, replacing SportSoft effective immediately.

## Background

SportSoft served RRROCA's membership and program registration needs but was showing limitations in user experience, payment processing, and integration with modern tools. Communal offers:

- **Integrated membership management** — online registration, renewals, and family memberships
- **Event registration** — community events, programs, and volunteer sign-ups
- **Payment processing** — Stripe integration for secure online payments
- **Community engagement** — built-in communication tools and member directories

## Financial Impact

- **SportSoft:** Annual licensing cost included in operating budget
- **Communal:** Transaction-based pricing, no upfront licensing fee
- **Net impact:** Cost-neutral to cost-positive depending on transaction volume

## Outcome

✅ **Motion passed** — February 2026

Communal has been operational since adoption and is now the primary platform for:
- Membership registration and renewals
- Community event registration
- Safety program sign-ups (e.g., Visioneers Block Watch)
- Payment processing via Stripe

> This motion is presented for formal ratification. The platform was adopted by board consensus in February 2026 and has been in active use since.
