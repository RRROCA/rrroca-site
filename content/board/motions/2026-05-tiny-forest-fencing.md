## Motion

**Be it resolved** that the Board of Directors approve an unplanned expenditure of up to **$1,200.00** (inclusive of taxes) for the supply and installation of perimeter fencing around the tiny forest planting bed at the Rocky Ridge community park.

## Background

The tiny forest project has been an important community initiative for park beautification and environmental stewardship. The existing perimeter protection (orange snow fencing) was destroyed during a recent wind storm and is no longer functional.

Dave Spencer (Parks portfolio) has arranged a quote from **Windscape** for supply and installation of permanent perimeter fencing at a cost of **$1,128.75** before taxes. The requested approval of $1,200 provides a small buffer for taxes and minor scope adjustments.

## Rationale

- The current orange snow fence has been obliterated by wind and is ineffective
- Permanent fencing protects the planting bed investment and young trees
- The vendor quote is competitive and has been arranged by the Parks portfolio lead
- This is a one-time capital expenditure with no ongoing cost implications
